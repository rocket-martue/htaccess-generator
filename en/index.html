preload requires includeSubDomains and max-age of at least 1 year (31536000)

This flag signals intent to be added to browser preload lists. Even with preload enabled, it will not appear in output if includeSubDomains is OFF. If using preload, enable includeSubDomains and set max-age to at least 1 year (31536000). Once registered, removal can take months — do not enable unless your HTTPS setup is complete.

Before applying CSP to production, check for violations in the browser DevTools (F12) → Console tab and adjust to match your site.
